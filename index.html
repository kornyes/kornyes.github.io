ELIZABETH . TARAKANOVA

I was born on April 27, 2000 in Dnipro, Ukraine. From childhood I dreamed of becoming a singer or an astronaut, but it so happened that my parents sent me to music school, thus connecting my life with music, singing and playing the piano. From the age of 7 to 14, I went to music school every day, listened to classical music and went to vocal and choral competitions. But over time, I began to reach for art, because from an early age I loved to draw, and after graduating from music school I went to take exams at the school of art. All 4 years after school I went to draw, in the end I received a certificate and accidentally went to the website of the Academy of Arts, and realized that I want to study there. This is my third year as a graphic designer, and I have many interesting subjects with which I`ll connect all my life.
